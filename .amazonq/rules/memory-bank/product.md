# AeroLedger Product Overview

## Purpose
AeroLedger is a luxury aviation platform that combines flight booking, private jet sales, and tokenized asset trading with cryptocurrency payment integration. It enables high-net-worth individuals to purchase aviation services and assets using Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, USDC, Solana, and BNB.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Crypto-Native Aviation**: First-class aviation services with seamless cryptocurrency payments
- **Dual Business Model**: Flight bookings + private jet sales + tokenized asset marketplace
- **Multi-Chain Support**: 6 cryptocurrencies across Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and BSC networks
- **Luxury Experience**: Premium UI/UX with 3D visualizations and real-time portfolio tracking

## Key Features

### 1. Cryptocurrency Payment System
- **Supported Currencies**: BTC, ETH, USDT, USDC, SOL, BNB
- **Payment Flow**: Initialize → Send to wallet → Confirm with transaction hash
- **Automatic Pricing**: 15% markup (10% profit + 5% service fee)
- **Multi-Chain**: Ethereum, Bitcoin, Solana, Binance Smart Chain

### 2. Flight Booking Platform
- Commercial and private flight search
- Real-time availability
- Crypto payment integration
- Booking management and tracking

### 3. Private Jet Sales
- 6 luxury aircraft models ($9.5M - $90M range)
- Detailed specifications and 3D models
- Direct purchase with cryptocurrency
- Ownership transfer via blockchain

### 4. Tokenized Asset Marketplace
- Fractional ownership of luxury assets
- Real-time portfolio tracking
- Asset trading and liquidity
- Transaction history and analytics

### 5. User Management
- Secure authentication with JWT
- Role-based access (user/admin)
- Auto-registration on login
- Admin role detection (email-based)

### 6. Admin Dashboard
- Real-time statistics and analytics
- Booking management
- Payment tracking and verification
- User activity monitoring

### 7. Currency Calculator
- Real-time crypto price conversion
- Support for all 6 cryptocurrencies
- USD to crypto calculations
- Live market data integration

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- **High-Net-Worth Individuals**: Crypto holders seeking luxury aviation services
- **Crypto Investors**: Users looking to diversify into real-world assets
- **Business Travelers**: Executives preferring crypto payments for corporate travel

### Secondary Users
- **Aviation Brokers**: Facilitating private jet transactions
- **Asset Managers**: Managing tokenized aviation portfolios
- **Platform Administrators**: Monitoring operations and payments

## Use Cases

### Flight Booking
1. User searches for flights (commercial or private)
2. Selects preferred flight and payment method (crypto)
3. Receives wallet address and exact amount
4. Sends payment and submits transaction hash
5. Booking confirmed after payment verification

### Private Jet Purchase
1. User browses marketplace of luxury aircraft
2. Views detailed specifications and 3D models
3. Initiates purchase with cryptocurrency
4. Completes payment through multi-chain wallet
5. Ownership transferred via smart contract

### Asset Trading
1. User explores tokenized aviation assets
2. Purchases fractional ownership shares
3. Tracks portfolio performance in real-time
4. Trades assets on secondary market
5. Receives dividends or appreciation gains

### Admin Operations
1. Monitor platform statistics (bookings, payments, users)
2. Verify payment transactions
3. Manage booking confirmations
4. Track revenue and profit margins
5. Generate reports and analytics

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams
- **Flight Bookings**: 15% markup on all bookings
- **Jet Sales**: Commission on private jet transactions
- **Asset Trading**: Transaction fees on tokenized assets
- **Service Fees**: 5% service fee on all crypto payments

### Profit Structure
- 10% profit margin on bookings
- 5% service fee for payment processing
- Commission-based jet sales
- Trading fees on asset marketplace
